Shrimp Alfredo

Delicious shrimp wrapped in a bed of savory garlic alfredo noodles will make for an unforgettable luncheon!

    Ingredients
  • Shrimp
  • Fettucine Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Garlic
    Recipe
  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Melt 1/4 teaspon butter in a pan and the garlic cloves.
  3. Add shrimp to pan once garlic is softened.
  4. Drain pasta, and add heavy cream and alredo sauce.
  5. Add shrimp to pasta, stir, and add parmesean cheese.
  6. Enjoy!
