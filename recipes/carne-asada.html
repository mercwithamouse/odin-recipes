Carne Asadai Tacos

Carne asada tacos are made from flank steak, tortillas, onion, meltable cheese blend, fresh lime juice, and salsa verde.

  • Flank Steak
  • Sweet Onion
  • Favorite Cheese Blend
  • Lime Juice
  • Salsa Verde
  • Tortillas
    1. Sweat onions on a pan in a small drizzle of oil.
    2. Add flank steak to pan, allowing the bottom to sear.
    3. Flip flank steak when sides are browning and bottom is browned
    4. When medium rare, cut steak into bitable size strips.
    5. Add tortillas to a clean pan on low-medium heat.
    6. When crisp, remove tortillas from pan.
    7. Place strips onto tortillas.
    8. Season with cheese, lime juice, and salsa.
    9. Enjoy!
