Peanut Butter and Jelly

A childhood classic updated for your adult pallete!

    Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
    Steps
  1. Grab 2 slices of bread
  2. Apply peanut butter to one side of one of the slices. Spread evenly
  3. Apply jelly to one side of the other slice. Spread evenly
  4. Flip one slice and place it on top of the other slice.
  5. Enjoy!
